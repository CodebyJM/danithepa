Our Services

We offer 100% satisafaction and money back guarantee

Mock Interview

• The interview process is one of the most important aspects of gaining acceptance into PA school. You impressed the admission committee on paper, now you must impress them in person!

• I will provide you with a 20 minute personalized interview process via zoom catered to the school of your choice! I will be hand-selecting questions relevant to your specific program.

• I will provide constructive feedback at the end so you can be confident walking into your interview!

• Having a mock interview is crucical for eliminating those nerves!

Zoom Call

• One-hour zoom session answering any questions you may have related to PA school or the application process.

• May discuss your overall application including strengths and weaknesses, statistics, resume, CASPA, GRE strategies, interview tips and much more.

• Be sure to have questions prepared so that we can use the more time effectively!

• (Most frequently purchased)

Personal Statement Review

• Amongst the many other challenges of applying to PA school, writing a personal statement is a difficult and intimidating process. You want to write a paper that conveys your character, motivation and qualities that separate you apart from the rest of the applicants.

• Myself, along with other editors, will assist you in delivering a personal statement with a clear, concise and professional message.

Resume Review

• Resumes are often the first impression for admission committees. It is an important tool because it offers only a page or two to display your top skills, qualities, background and education.

• I will help you create a resume that grabs the attention of the admission committee by selling your strongest skills and accomplishments.
